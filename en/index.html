TokyoADN

Tokyo App.net is a community of users on App.net who live in Tokyo and elsewhere in the Kantou area of Japan.

Users gather in events planned by the community. Membership or registration is not required. The details of the next event will soon be posted.

Follow @tokyoadn and #tokyoadn on App.net to know the latest updates about Tokyo App.net. You may also follow @tokyoadn and #tokyoadn on Twitter.

This site is generated using Middleman and its code is shared on GitHub for anyone to fork and contribute. This community site and its related assets are managed by @remino (a.k.a. @edojin).
